OBJECTIVES


Pro-life and pro-family advocacy is centered on safeguarding human life from conception to natural death while simultaneously strengthening the family unit as the cornerstone of society. This multifaceted approach encompasses legislative action, public education, support services, and grassroots mobilization to create a culture that cherishes life and supports families at every stage.

About Us


ROMIJA Women Life and Family Foundation Ltd. (RWLFAMLtd) is a non-governmental organization (NGO) dedicated to empowering women, advocating for pro-life and pro-family values, and supporting vulnerable members of society. Founded by female lawyers from diverse religious backgrounds, ROMIJA seeks to address the challenges facing women and families in Africa by promoting education, healthcare, and economic opportunities. Our mission is to create a world where the sanctity of life is upheld, families are strengthened, and women are empowered to lead fulfilling lives.


Our Story


We began with a simple mission: to empower women and advocate for pro-life and pro-family values.
