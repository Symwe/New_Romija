OJECTIVES


Pro-life and pro-family advocacy is centered on safeguarding human life from conception to natural death while simultaneously strengthening the family unit as the cornerstone of society. This multifaceted approach encompasses legislative action, public education, support services, and grassroots mobilization to create a culture that cherishes life and supports families at every stage.


Advocating against abortion.

At ROMIJA we oppose against abortion and we work to raise awareness about its consequences.


Forming pro-life & pro-family clubs in schools, universities n institutions

About Us


Romija Women Life and Family Foundation Ltd. (RWLFAMLtd) is a non-governmental organization (NGO) dedicated to empowering women, advocating for pro-life and pro-family values, and supporting vulnerable members of society.Founded by female lawyers from diverse religious backgrounds. We leverage the power of legal expertise and compassionate action to create positive change.
